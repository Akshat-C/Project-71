The school was getting a new building to accomodate the growing amount of students and more advanced classrooms. The construction of the new build began in 2018.

support unit

new hall

Completed in beginning of 2021

It was created to allow for more space when the school gathered. It also had a massive screen which allowed pupils sitting in the back of the room to see the screen with ease. It also has a stage.

Zone Cross Country

School Came 13th Place Out of 21 Overall

Students from grades 2-6 participated in the Zone Cross Country and represented their school. The school performed extremely well and one grade 6 student came 9th place out of around a 100 students in the 3km race.

what's new

  • New School Build

    This new build was only completed a few weeks ago. It is equipped with advanced classrooms, a new library and a new computer lab. The building is for all students from grades 1-6 and has 3 floors. It also has an ampitheatre were the classes can line up.

  • Online School

    All of Sydney is in lockdown so students will be working from home for the foreseeable future. Only children of essential workers are permitted to come to school. For the students at school there will be teachers supervising them.
